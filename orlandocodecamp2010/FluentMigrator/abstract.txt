Sick of sql change scripts? Driving yourself insane trying to version your database? Say hello to FluentMigrator. FluentMigrator is an open source library used for versioning your database using a domain specific language in c#. It was originally developed by Nate Kohari, Justin Etheredge and Sean Chambers as a port from another Migration library. Come learn how to easily and quickly version your database and free yourself from database change management.
